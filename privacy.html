Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your data.

Last Updated: October 4, 2025

Important: Our tools process all data locally in your browser. Your text, files, and other input never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to save or share them.

1. Information We Collect

Our tools are designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information to provide our services:

Text and File Input

The text, data, and files you input into our tools are processed entirely in your web browser. This means:

  • Your data never gets sent to our servers
  • No permanent storage of your input on our systems
  • Complete control over your data at all times

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain information when you visit our site:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our services
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, and IP address
  • Cookies: Essential cookies for website functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: Operating and maintaining our tools
  • Improving User Experience: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance functionality
  • Website Analytics: Understanding how visitors use our site
  • Security: Preventing abuse and ensuring service integrity
  • Communication: Responding to your inquiries and feedback

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Processing

All tool processing happens locally in your web browser using JavaScript. Your data:

  • Is processed entirely on your device
  • Never transmitted to our servers
  • Remains under your complete control

Temporary Storage

For better user experience, some tools may temporarily cache data in your browser's local storage:

  • This data remains on your device only
  • You can clear this data anytime using browser settings
  • No sensitive information is stored permanently

Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Secure hosting infrastructure

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Analytics

We use analytics services to understand how our tools are used and to improve user experience. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to Access: Request information about what data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing
  • Right to Portability: Request your data in a portable format

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Where appropriate, providing additional notification

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us:
